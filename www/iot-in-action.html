Microsoft IoT

I managed workflow and owned creative for the IoT in Action team from 2017-2018. I have extensive experience working with corporate branding guidelines and delivering best in class creative to land key messages.

The goal for the team was growing registrations and engagement through an event series and through a webinar initiative. I worked with the client to revise and update brand guidelines, update copy, and launch various support campaigns. IoT in Action brought various parties from across the business ecosystem to unify them towards building new solutions. We adhered to Microsoft Brand guidelines while differentiating the IoT in Action brand internationally.

I handled logistics for the IoT events in San Francisco in 2018 and managed vendor relationships. We also launched numerous registration sites and one-off event sites to support engagement across the brand, and grew registrations and engagement YoY.
