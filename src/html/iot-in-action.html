IoT in Action

I worked closely with the IoT in Action team for over a year, working with numerous key stakeholders throughout the organization. I have extensive experience working with corporate branding guidelines and delivering best in class creative to land key messages.

The goal for the team was growing registrations and engagement through an event series and through a webinar initiative. I worked with the client to revise and update brand guidelines, update copy, and launch various support campaigns. The branding was aimed to be aspirational while being accessible. It sought to bring together many different parties from across the business ecosystem and unify them towards collaboration utilizing Microsoft platform solutions. We adhered to Microsoft Brand guidelines while differentiating the IoT in Action brand internationally. I handled logistics for the IoT events in San Francisco in 2018 and managed vendor relationships.

We also launched numerous registration sites and one-off event sites to support engagement across the brand, and successfully grew registrations and engagement YoY.
